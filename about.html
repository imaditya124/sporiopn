About The Sporion

Sporion 2.0 is an sports event organized by Engineering college Jhalawar held from 4th of March to 7th of March including games like volleyball, basketball,tug of war, Carrom,long jump, jevelin throw, kabaddi,shotput,chess and badminton.The word sporion is made up of two words sports and Orion. Orion representing a constellation that looks like hunter having four stars representing coordination, unity, discipline and spirit.All teams were competing for the single title hunter and gave their best& healthy competition, Spirit was maintained during the event something it's not about to win it's all about to experience that you gain.Sports teaches us discipline.The value of time , togetherness and unity..
